History

Founded in 1995, RAPIDS started as a small group of river lovers with a big dream. Our goal was to create unforgettable adventures for people of all ages and experience levels.

Through years of dedication, we have grown into a trusted name in white water rafting. Safety, excitement, and nature have been the pillars of our journey ever since.
